# Welcome!

I am an incoming PhD student in Finance at the [Swiss Finance Institute – Università della Svizzera italiana (USI Lugano)](https://sfi.usi.ch), starting Fall 2026.

Currently, I work as a Research Associate in the Monetary and Economic Department at the [Bank for International Settlements (BIS)](https://www.bis.org), where I focus on macroeconomic analysis. Prior to this, I was a Junior Researcher in the Structural Economic Research Department at the [Central Bank of the Republic of Türkiye](https://www.tcmb.gov.tr/wps/wcm/connect/en/tcmb+en).

I hold an M.Sc. in Economics from the [University of Bonn](https://www.uni-bonn.de/en), where I specialized in financial economics and worked as a Research Assistant at the [Institute of Finance and Statistics](https://www.ifs.uni-bonn.de).

My research interests lie in monetary policy, empirical banking and international finance.
