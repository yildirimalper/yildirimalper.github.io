About Me

I am currently a graduate student in APE at the Paris School of Economics, with a strong focus on macroeconomics and financial economics. I completed my master's degree in Economics at the Rheinische Friedrich-Wilhelms-Universität Bonn. I serve as a Research Assistant at the Institute of Finance and Statistics, working closely with Dr. Janko Heineken on the dynamics of heterogeneous expectations and stock market cycles, and as a Teaching Assistant in "International Monetary Economics" at Université Paris 1 Panthéon-Sorbonne.

My research interests lie in monetary policy, financial stability and macroeconomics.
