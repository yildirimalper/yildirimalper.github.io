About Me

I am currently a Researcher at the Structural Economic Research Division of the Central Bank of the Republic of Türkiye. I hold a master's degree in Economics from the Rheinische Friedrich-Wilhelms-Universität Bonn, where I specialized in financial economics and applied microeconomics. Previously, I worked as a Research Assistant at the Institute of Finance and Statistics, collaborating with Dr. Janko Heineken on the dynamics of heterogeneous expectations in household finance.

My research interests lie in monetary economics, empirical banking and microeconometrics.
