About Me

I am currently an Associate at the Monetary and Economic Department of the Bank for International Settlements (BIS), and on leave from my position as a Researcher at the Structural Economic Research Department at the Central Bank of the Republic of Türkiye. I hold a master's degree in Economics from the Rheinische Friedrich-Wilhelms-Universität Bonn, where I specialized in financial economics and applied microeconomics. Previously, I worked as a Research Assistant at the Institute of Finance and Statistics collaborating with Dr. Janko Heineken.

My research interests lie in monetary economics, empirical banking, and microeconometrics.
