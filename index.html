About Me

I am currently a master's student in Economics at Rheinische Friedrich-Wilhelms-Universität Bonn, with a strong focus on financial economics. I serve as a Research Assistant at the Institute of Finance and Statistics, working closely with Dr. Janko Heineken on the intriguing dynamics of heterogenous expectations and stock market cycles.

My research interests lie in financial stability, monetary policy transmission, and corporate finance.
