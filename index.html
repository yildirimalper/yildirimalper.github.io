About Me

I am an Associate in the Monetary and Economic Department at the Bank for International Settlements (BIS), currently on leave from my role as a Junior Researcher in the Structural Economic Research Department at the Central Bank of the Republic of Türkiye.

I hold an M.Sc. in Economics from the University of Bonn, with a focus on financial economics. Previously, I was a Research Assistant at the Institute of Finance and Statistics, where I worked with Dr. Janko Heineken.

My research interests lie in monetary policy, international macroeconomics, and empirical banking.
